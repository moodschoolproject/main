What is Sign Language?

Sign language is a language that is used to communicate with people who are blind or have limited vision. It is a form of communication that is used to communicate with people who are unable to see or hear. Many countries have their own variations of sign language. A few examples are: American Sign Language (ASL), Pakistani Sign Language (PSL), and British Sign Language (BSL).
